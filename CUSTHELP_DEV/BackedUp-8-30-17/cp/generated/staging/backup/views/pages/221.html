Left an item at the gate prior to departure or in the terminal?

Left an item onboard one of our planes? 
Please contact the station that you arrived at, our Baggage Service office numbers are listed below.   Items left on the aircraft are held at the station's baggage service office for 5 days.  After 5 days they are sent to our Central Baggage Department. Maximum item retention time is 45 days after which the item will be donated.

If you called the station's number below and your item was not turned into the arrival station, please fill out our lost and found form at https://www.virginamerica.com/lostandfound.  

This form is managed by our Central Baggage department which receives all left on board items after 5 days.  There is no need to call.  If we have a match for your item we will contact you immediately.

Virgin America is not responsible for transporting, holding or reuniting these items back to guests. However, we will do our best to help reunite you with your item.
